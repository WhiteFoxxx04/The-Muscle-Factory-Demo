About The Muscle Factory

The Muscle Factory was founded by brothers Satyajit and Devashish Chourasia in 1991 with an objective to provide for a better fitness option to fitness enthusiast. Personalized Service, Expertise and Result Oriented Training became the trade mark of the gym and fetched popularity and recognition.

"Since the day we started our first gym it has been a constant learning experience. Handling thousands of members having different body types, metabolic systems, limitations and problems have enriched our experience."
- Satyajit Chourasia.


"Human Body is God's most beautiful creation. Fitness is dedication and discipline that takes you closer to god who is within us. If we pray our bodies god will give us health and with it we can enjoy the goodness of nature... and our world."
- Devashish Chourasia.


Muscle Factory will be taking the next step in becoming a worldwide gym by opening 12 new locations all over the world in 2021.
